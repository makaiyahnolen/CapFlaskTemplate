Feeling stuck is very normal, especially throughout your highschool years. There's so much work, stress and drama to deal with and you don't know how to manage it all. We've all been there before. To start off, you need a planner to decrease some of that stress and overload. Start by writing your schedule down and your goals for the day. That way you can tackle your goals are for the day or week. Staying organized is on of the key way to get through highschool because it prevents you from getting stuck and losing your train of thought.
